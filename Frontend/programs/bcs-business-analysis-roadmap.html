Build your BA and architecture journey step by step

Follow a structured pathway from foundational business analysis skills to enterprise architecture, agile delivery, security assurance, data governance, and project leadership.

01 Foundation

BCS Foundation Certificate in Business Analysis

Foundational analysis principles, stakeholder understanding, and requirements basics.

Focus: Start your business analysis career with core BA methods.

02 Practitioner

BCS Practitioner Certificate in Business Analysis Practice

Advanced business analysis techniques and practical application frameworks.

Focus: Apply BA practices to real organizational change scenarios.

03 Architecture

BCS Practitioner Certificate in Enterprise and Solution Architecture

Enterprise architecture and solution design principles for strategic alignment.

Focus: Link business objectives to architecture and solution outcomes.

04 Agile

Professional Certificate in Agile Business Analysis

Agile BA techniques for iterative product and delivery environments.

Focus: Strategic business architecture and transformation planning.
